  • Click here for league registration information

    League resigtration includes:
    1. Filling an online personal information form
    2. Uploading a photo of the player's ID and a passport picture
    3. Paying a one-time 70 NIS registration fee to the league
    4. Uploading of a picture of a medical test by an approved medical institue

    Additional details may be obtained under "General info", in the league's letter to the parents and players     League registration page (Hebrew) Medical test upload form (Hebrew)

  • Click here for participation payment information

    Please only pay the participation payment after receiving your loaned personal equipment and the team's manager's approval!

    Participation payment includes:
    1. A security check given to the team manager for 1500 NIS after receiving your loaned personal equipment in full. This check is given back to the player upon returning his equipment at the end of the season.
    2. The participation payment of 2700 NIS paid either by credit card through a secure online form, OR by cash, in six(6) payments of 300 NIS, plus another payment of 900 NIS.
    The online credit card payment option is divided into 9 monthly payments of 300 NIS each.

    Additional information may be found under "General information", in the team's letter to the parents.     Secure payment page (Hebrew) Please input a valid E-mail address to which receipts will be automatically sent.
    Please notice the participation payment is a seasonal payment and does not reflect nor is tied to the team's activity period, and is divided into smaller payments for the convenient of the parents!
